🚩 About Me

Hi! I'm Huiqiang Rong (戎惠强), a Master's student in Computer Science and Technology at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT), advised by Associate Prof. Yifan Zhu. I obtained my Bachelor of Engineering degree in Computer Science and Technology from BUPT as well.

My research interests include:

  • Large Language Models (LLMs)
  • Hallucination Control & Mitigation
  • Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs)
  • AI for Education (AI4Edu)
